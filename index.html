The Hiring Problem

Hiring teams are expected to move fast, hire fairly, and prove quality of hire — but most workflows still run on PDFs, gut feel, and disconnected tools. SkillMirror is positioned to solve this by turning hiring activity into a structured intelligence system that scales with the business.

Where Hiring Breaks Today

  • Resume screening is keyword-heavy, not capability-aware.
  • Interview feedback is inconsistent across interviewers.
  • Hiring decisions are hard to justify to leadership.
  • Great candidates drop due to slow loop completion.

How SkillMirror Repositions the Process
