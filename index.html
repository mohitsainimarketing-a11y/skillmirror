The Hiring Problem

Organizations evaluate candidates using resumes, interviews and references, but these signals are inconsistent and difficult to compare. As companies scale, hiring becomes slower, subjective and increasingly dependent on individual judgment rather than structured evidence.

Fragmented Signals

Recruiters receive resumes in different formats with varying levels of detail. Important capabilities may be hidden while irrelevant keywords dominate early screening decisions.

→

Structured Hiring Intelligence

SkillMirror transforms job descriptions and candidate resumes into structured capability signals, allowing hiring teams to compare candidates objectively and focus on evidence rather than intuition.
